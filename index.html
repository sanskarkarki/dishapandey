About Me

I'm a leather designer with a passion for storytelling through fashion. My work fuses heritage craftsmanship with bold, modern aesthetics—ranging from hand-moulded corsets to tech-driven digital creations.

Projects

Gulbadan Corset

Inspired by Mughal elegance and floral motifs, this hand-moulded corset in burgundy leather tells the story of feminine strength and silent rebellion.

Paint Her Black

Inspired by The Rolling Stones, this bold red corset and black flared pants express heartbreak and strength through contrasting colors and rock-inspired design.

Crimson Bloom

This hibiscus-inspired leather bow dress combines voluminous elegance with structural strength in coral tones and embroidery.

Blossoms Jacket

A pink leather jacket inspired by sakura blossoms and geometric symmetry, featuring hand embroidery and appliqué techniques.
